EDUCATION KNOWLEDGE BASE

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and habits through learning, teaching, training, and research. Education helps individuals develop critical thinking, problem solving abilities, and social understanding.

Types of Education:
1. Primary Education – Basic schooling for children.
2. Secondary Education – High school level education.
3. Higher Education – College and university level education.

Benefits of Education:
- Improves knowledge and skills
- Increases employment opportunities
- Promotes innovation and creativity
- Supports economic development

Modern Education Technologies:
- Online learning platforms
- Artificial Intelligence in education
- Virtual classrooms
- Learning management systems
